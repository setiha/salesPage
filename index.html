Hello!!

We are Víctor and Laura and we have designed and created this `dream´ together. We know how you feel when you think that you will never be able to be fluent in a new language because it happened to us too.
By the time we met, we had both lived in many different countries such us: England, USA, Spain, India, Greece, Mexico, Portugal, Qatar.... And we have experienced many times the feeling you get when you just cannot express your basic needs or the sense of loneliness you may know, for not being able to make new friends because the foreign or native people you meet don't want to waste their time communicating with you barely speaking their language.
At the moment, we both can speak four languages and we are more than happy to have fulfilled boundaries that once we thought were impossible. We embraced the learning process knowing that it would take time, effort and strength and feeling a great sympathy for our students. We are now sharing our experience, knowledge and personal tips with many travellers from all over the world who come to Gozo to learn, discover and have a good time with us.
At Ta Óniera we have thought and worked to create an amazing experience which combines learning English while enjoying your holidays in the Mediterranean island of Gozo, Malta, where English is the second official language. You will be exposed to the English language and have fun being engaged in the great number of activities that Gozo offers. Dedicated to English students like you, who don't feel like spending their vacations in the typical student´s destinations, we have designed a personalised English method making sure that you will learn and live it up because …

you will learn and live it up because …

The flexibility is total: you set your goals, pace, kind of course… diving in the morning?, hiking after lunch?, going on a kayak excursion?, painting at night?, renting a boat for the whole day?, going climbing? That is Ok with us! You are on holiday!

We will help you in every step of the process with anything you need and we will make sure your English programme is adapted to your final objective.

Ta Óneira is a small project, so you will receive personal attention. Not only in the classroom but also after the bell rings. We will show you the hidden treasures of the island and strengthen your confidence to meet and talk to the local and international community who live in Gozo.

Our certified teachers are always approachable and respond to your needs whether it is a grammatical question or where to dine this evening.

Studying with us is a whole experience, not just another English course. We will guide you through the learning process focusing on the use of English and encouraging you to practice as much as possible.
